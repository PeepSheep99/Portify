# PlaylistCopier

## What This Is

A tool that lets users migrate their playlists and liked songs between music streaming services (Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, etc.). Solves the pain of losing curated music collections when switching platforms.

## Core Value

Users can preserve their music curation when switching between any major streaming platform.
